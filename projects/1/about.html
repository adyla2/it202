About Superior Dog Walkers

Superior Dog Walkers are leading professionals in the dog walking and pet sitting business.

Not only do we provide constant contact with current clients, through phone, text, and pictures, we also have GPS tracking available. Tracking allows clients to view the routes we take with your dog on a daily basis.

Other services are also provided, including personalized house/pet sitting, which we have found to be ideal for clients and their pets to rest easy. The dogs are always more confortable and less stressed when kept in their own homes.
