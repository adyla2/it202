Using API's and Google Maps

Potholes here in Chicago are a BIG problem. Potholes are the worst. Here I pull in data from a government website to populate data tables. We loop through all the pothole data for the city of Chicago and return only the open requests (potholes that haven't been filled yet!) and the neighborhood code they are located in. Three types of data display are shown, including a heatmap, which uses longitude and latitude data. Click here for Pothole Data for City of Chicago.
